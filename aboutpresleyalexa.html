Presley Alexa aims to encompass the ‘rockstar girlfriend’ aesthetic; the ultimate cool girl, the life of the party, and the girl who turns heads with her impeccable style. Designers draw most inspiration from 90's street style and iconic rock star looks in order to create a capsule wardrobe that will never go out of style. The ultimate goal of the brand is to bring luxury coolness into everyday style in jewelry, outerwear, and clothing to elevate every moment.
